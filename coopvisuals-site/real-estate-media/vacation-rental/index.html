A different kind of shoot

This is not a listing photo shoot.

A listing photo shoot is documentation. The goal is to show square footage, room count, and condition. A vacation rental shoot is an invitation.


Guests don't scroll Airbnb comparing bedrooms. They look at photos and ask: can I picture myself there? That requires more images, a different composition style, and a completely different editorial eye.


Detail shots of the coffee maker, the tile, the throw blanket. The pool getting its own moment. The outdoor kitchen shot like it's a magazine spread. The view from the bed, not just the room from the doorway.


It's not clinical documentation — it's emotional connection. And that's what drives bookings.

The bottom line
Better photos are the highest-ROI upgrade a vacation rental owner can make. Every dollar spent on photography returns in bookings.
