Web Design — Payment & Project Terms

5-Page Websites

No deposit required. We build your site and provide a full preview — free of charge — so you can review it before committing. Full payment of the one-time setup fee is due only when you're ready to publish and connect the site to your domain.

10-Page & 15-Page Websites

A 50% deposit is required after your discovery call to reserve your project slot and begin work. The remaining 50% balance is due before the completed site is connected to your domain and published. No exceptions.

Your site will not be connected to your domain or made publicly accessible until all outstanding balances are paid in full, regardless of project size.
